Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about travel with TravelEase

You can change or cancel your flight booking by logging into your TravelEase account and accessing the "My Bookings" section. From there, select the booking you wish to modify and follow the prompts. Please note that change and cancellation fees may apply depending on the airline's policy and fare rules.

If you need assistance, our customer service team is available 24/7 to help you with changes or cancellations.

Our refund policy depends on the type of booking and the specific terms of your fare:

  • Refundable fares: Full refund minus any applicable fees
  • Non-refundable fares: No refund, but you may receive a credit for future travel (minus fees)
  • Hotel bookings: Varies by property (check cancellation policy at time of booking)
  • Packages: Typically refundable up to a certain date before travel

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days to the original form of payment.

You can add baggage to your flight booking by:

  1. Logging into your TravelEase account
  2. Going to "My Bookings" and selecting your flight
  3. Clicking on "Add Baggage" or "Manage Booking"
  4. Selecting the baggage options you need
  5. Completing the payment process

You can also add baggage at the airport, but it's usually more expensive than purchasing in advance.

Our customer service team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions or issues:

  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567
  • Email: support@travelease.com
  • Live Chat: Available on our website and mobile app
  • Social Media: @TravelEase on Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram

For faster service, have your booking reference number ready when contacting us.
